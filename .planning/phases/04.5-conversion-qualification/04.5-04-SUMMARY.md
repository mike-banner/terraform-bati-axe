### server/api/v1/admin/paywall-analytics.get.ts (NEW)
Admin-only (403 on non-admin). Fetches last 30 days of paywall_events, computes:
- `totals`: counts by event_type
- `qualified_funnel`: same filtered to qualify_score >= 3 + conversion_rate
- `recent`: last 50 events for activity feed
