## Overall Direction

**Exaggerated Minimalism & Bento Grids**
The project uses a premium, highly structured visual language relying on 12-column Bento grids for data density without clutter. The color palette is anchored in Industrial Grey (#64748B) and Slate-50 for structure, punctuated by Safety Orange (#F97316) for high-conversion CTAs. Typography is massive and contrasting (Satoshi/DM Sans). Micro-interactions (like hover elevations) bring the UI to life, acting as the foundation for further Framer Motion/21st.dev animations.
</design_direction>
